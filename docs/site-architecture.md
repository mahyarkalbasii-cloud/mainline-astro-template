## Home page section intent

### 1) Hero
Goal:
- explain what PersianSaze is
- define market coverage
- establish the main value proposition
- present primary CTA

### 2) مسئله بازار
Goal:
- explain why selling in the construction market is difficult
- frame the cost of scattered projects, weak data, and bad timing

### 3) راه‌حل پرشین‌سازه
Goal:
- present the 3-part value proposition:
  - داده زنده
  - ابزارهای اجرایی
  - آموزش

### 4) چطور کار می‌کند
Goal:
- show the product as a structured system, not just a list of projects
- highlight:
  - data collection and updates
  - execution layer
  - AI layer

### 5) مراحل استفاده
Goal:
- show the user workflow from discovery to follow-up
- keep it operational and easy to scan

### 6) مراحل ساخت قابل پوشش
Goal:
- show the construction stages covered by the system
- clarify breadth of usage

### 7) چه کسانی از پرشین‌سازه استفاده می‌کنند
Goal:
- segment the audience into:
  - fast/standard sales
  - consultative/specialized sales

### 8) تعرفه‌ها / اشتراک‌ها
Goal:
- present the 4 plans clearly
- explain coverage differences
- support lead generation for plan selection

### 9) جمع‌بندی ارزش پیشنهادی
Goal:
- reinforce what all subscriptions include
- reduce hesitation before CTA

### 10) FAQ
Goal:
- answer common objections
- support SEO/AEO

### 11) Footer
Goal:
- provide utility links, contact points, and trust baseline
